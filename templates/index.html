About the City Name Generator

This free city name generator helps writers, game designers, and world builders create believable city and town names for any setting. The random mode draws from 2,000+ curated names across four styles: Fantasy, Medieval, Sci-Fi, and Ancient.

The AI mode uses a language model to craft unique city names tailored to your world, including lore notes on linguistic roots and what the name evokes. Describe your city's geography, culture, or role and receive names like Ashenvorn or Vexion-7 with full explanation.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the city name generator good for?
World building for novels, D&D campaigns, video games, tabletop RPGs, and any creative project that needs believable fictional place names.
How are the fantasy city names created?
Fantasy names use elvish, arcane, and epic-sounding syllable combinations inspired by Tolkien, D&D, and high fantasy traditions. Medieval names use Anglo-Saxon, Germanic, and Latin roots similar to real English place names.
Can I generate sci-fi planet or station names?
Yes — the Sci-Fi style produces names suitable for space colonies, orbital stations, megacities, and distant planets.
